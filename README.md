<h2><a class="anchor" id="overview"></a>📖 Overview</h2>
The Global Electronic Retail Sales Analysis project focuses on analyzing a multinational electronics retail business using historical sales data collected across multiple countries, stores, products, customers, and sales channels.Using Python, the raw datasets were cleaned, transformed, and prepared for analysis. After preprocessing, the cleaned data was imported into Power BI, where interactive dashboards were created to monitor key performance indicators, identify sales trends, compare online and offline performance, evaluate product profitability, and generate actionable business insights.

---
<h2><a class="anchor" id="business-problem"></a>🎯 Business Problem</h2>
Although the company generated $55.8 Million in total revenue over multiple years, the business experienced a significant decline in revenue, orders, and product demand during the most recent period.

- Several important business questions needed to be answered:
  - Why has revenue dropped significantly?
  - Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
  - Which brands perform well and which require improvement?
  - How do online and offline stores contribute to total revenue?
  - Which customer segments generate maximum sales?
  - Which product categories should receive more investment?
  - What business strategies can improve future growth?

<h2><a class="anchor" id=" key-findings"></a>Key-findings</h2>
Major Insights :-

 - Revenue peaked during 2019
 - Revenue declined 75.8% in 2021
 - Offline stores contribute 79.55% revenue
 - Desktops generate highest revenue
 - Male and Female customers contribute almost equally
 - Average Order Value increased by 4.5%
 - Delivery time improved
